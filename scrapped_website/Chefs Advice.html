How to Make an Omelette

  1. Crack 2-3 eggs into a bowl.
  2. Whisk the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and add a little butter.
  4. Pour in the eggs and cook gently, stirring occasionally.
  5. When almost set, add cheese or herbs if desired, fold, and serve.
